Privacy Policy for NFO BodyState

Effective date: June 29, 2026

NFO BodyState is designed to help users review personal health, recovery, and body state information in a simple and private way.

Information We Access

NFO BodyState may access health and wellness information that you explicitly allow through Apple Health and related permissions on your device.

How Information Is Used

The app uses your information to display trends, daily insights, and journal context inside the app experience.

Data Storage

Your journal entries and app data are intended to remain on your device unless otherwise stated in a future version of the app.

Data Sharing

NFO BodyState does not sell your personal information.

NFO BodyState does not share your personal health data with third parties except where required to provide functionality you explicitly use, or where required by law.

Permissions

You can control app permissions, including Health access, in your device settings at any time.

Children's Privacy

NFO BodyState is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page.
